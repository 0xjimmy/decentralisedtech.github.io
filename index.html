Who We Are

DecentralisedTech develop products and solutions that run on smart contracts, blockchains and other forms of distributed ledger technology.

Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) brings a new level of security, transparency and fairness to the way technology and people interact. Leveraging this technology you can benefit your company or project by removing the need for trust and central points of failure.

Security

Leveraging a public blockchain to run your infrastructure offers very secure environments to keep and manage records, as the large amount of nodes used for consensus in public blockchains such as Ethereum makes it impossible to cheat or modify information.

Crash Resistant

Distributed technology such as Blockchain runs on many servers and computers, meaning that it is almost impossible to shut down a large public network like Bitcoin or Ethereum. Infrastructure build on these networks will always be live.

Transparent

Your business or project can benefit from the ability to prove transparency with PGP Encryption and cryptographic fingerprints stored in immutable and unhackable databases - distributed ledger.

Open Source

At DecentralisedTech we use and build Open Source software and protocols, such as the Bitcoin and Ethereum public networks, which allow us to leverage the ability to know and verify the foundation of a platform is secure, and to edit and improve infrastructure freely.
